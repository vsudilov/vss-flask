The Gamma-ray Optical and Near-IR Detector

GROND is detector system that is mounted on a 2.2m telescope in La Silla Observatory, Chile. A large part of my time as a graduate student involved working with GROND. I worked with GROND by performing observations and data analysis on-site, and by implementing code to help operate it remotely.

GROND detects light simultaneously in 7 filters. Stars and other astronomical objects emit different amounts of light in these filters, and some physical properties can be measured by their relative contributions.
